Multi-Material Recycling Facilities

Acceptable items from Residents:

  • Aluminum (Cans, Foil, Pie Pans with no food residue)
  • Appliances / White Goods<(A/C Units, Fridge, Washer/Dryer)
  • Automotive Fluids & Oil Filters (Anti-Freeze, Motor Oil)
  • Baby Strollers & Child Car Seats & Toys
  • Books (Text, Hard or Soft Cover)
  • Cardboard
  • Cartons (Juice, Milk, Soup Cartons)
  • CDs / DVDs / VHS Tapes
  • Cell Phones
  • Chipboard & SBS Board (Cereal, Snack, Shoe Boxes)
  • Clothing & Shoes
  • Computer Equipment (CPU, Monitors, Laptop, Printers, Peripherals)
  • Cooking Oil
  • Electronics (Any corded electronic device)
  • Glass Bottles & Jars
  • Pallets
  • Paper (Mail, Magazines, Newspaper, Phone Books, Office Paper)
  • Plastic (Bottles & Containers, #1, #2, #3, #5)
  • Propane Tanks
  • Rigid Plastics (Buckets, Crates, Plastic Baskets, Lawn Furniture)
  • Scrap Metal (Bikes, Grills, Mowers, Pipes, Swing Sets, etc.)
  • Tires
